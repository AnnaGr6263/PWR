Who am I?

My name is Anna Grelewska and I am a student of Algorithmic Computer Science at the Wrocław University of Technology. My passion is programming, mathematics, and solving complex problems. I am currently in the 4th semester of my 2nd year of studies and also serve as the class representative. I actively engage in university life as a member of the Faculty Student Council.

I am a laureate of the Talent Study, a national mathematics competition organized by the Wrocław University of Technology.

IT Interests

Programming

My IT interests focus on algorithms, data analysis, and the applications of mathematics in computer science. I am passionate about programming in C++ and Java, designing and managing databases, and exploring computer architecture and operating systems. I believe that understanding the low-level mechanisms of hardware and software is key to creating optimal solutions. I continuously develop my skills to meet the challenges of modern technology.
